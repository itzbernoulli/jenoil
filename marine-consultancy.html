Marine Consultancy

Jenoil Integrated Services Limited’s Marine Consultancy Division is made-up of experts from various disciplines including environmental management and assessment, archaeology and geology through to economics, policy, project design and management. We have a proven track-record in the aggregate, renewable, oil and gas, government and research industries globally.


Jenoil Integrated Services Limited’s marine consultants are well placed to help clients meet their development aspirations, management responsibilities and consenting needs for offshore and coastal environments through tailor-made services. These encompass a multidisciplinary approach to the application of applied sciences and innovative technologies. We specialize in conducting technically complex and challenging projects in all aspects of our marine consultancy services. We have achieved an international reputation for environmental excellence by repeatedly exceeding clients' expectations. Central to our success is the desire to advocate innovative ideas and help find appropriate and cost-effective solutions that meet client needs. Our dedication ensures that clients receive quality services for the planning, design and project management of their projects.


Barge/Crew Boats Logistics


Jenoil Integrated Services Limited Marine’s operational, technical and safety procedures are revolutionary. The Company’s Marine & Logistics Division consistently employ the latest technology throughout our vessels nationwide, while driving the development of new technology in anticipation of our customers’ needs. Through strategic alliances, partnerships and exclusive management contracts, Jenoil Integrated Services Limited Marine is distinctively focused on remaining a premier transportation and offshore services company.


Jenoil Integrated Services Marine offers customers a comprehensive suite of support services including crew transportation, platform supply, offshore accommodation, maintenance support, standby safety services and anchor handling and mooring capabilities in both shallow and deepwater environments around the nation.


Other Major Marine Services include:

  • Barge management involving operations, repairs & maintenance
  • Full condition survey & appraisals of marine vessels/equipment
  • Operation of marine vessels e.g. platform supply vessels, anchor handling tug/supply vessels, deck barges, multi-purpose support vessels, bulk carries-crude oil tankers
