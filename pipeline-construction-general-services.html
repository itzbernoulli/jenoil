Pipeline Construction General Services

Jenoil Integrated Services provides general pipeline engineering services to clients in the commercial, industrial and municipal market segments. Functioning as the “toolbox” for a client’s own engineering organization, Jenoil Integrated Services can provide engineering assistance in the form of a feasibility study, construction and completion of a project, or “staff augmentation,” which is a request for Jenoil Integrated Services engineers to supplement the client’s existing engineering staff.


General services include:

  • Hydraulic Analysis
  • Feasibility & Economic Studies
  • Permitting Assistance
  • Inspection
  • Material Specification
  • Right-of-Way Acquisition Assistance
  • Construction Administration
  • Staff Augmentation

Integrity and Rehabilitation


Jenoil Integrated Services Limited strives to keep apprised of, and utilize, new technology and project management techniques in the rehabilitation of pipelines in Nigeria. Many of the vital pipeline transmission systems constructed during the last 4 to 5 years continue to be in operation today. As these underground utilities age, pipeline inspection requirements continue to evolve. We help companies evaluate the economic efficiencies and physical conditions of older pipelines to determine whether or not rehabilitation is necessary. Jenoil Integrated Services understands our clients’ project priorities, and aids in establishing the most cost- effective and operations-sensitive in-line inspection programs, customized to meet those needs.
