Property Development and Facility management

Jenoil Integrated Servicesprovides superior service in all aspects of real estate investment and management, as well as provides innovative and cost effective development solutions. We are a full service property management company, which includes leasing, rentals, maintenance, bill payment and financial reporting. We have an experienced and dedicated staff of property managers, leasing associates, maintenance and vendor managers, maintenance personnel and bookkeepers. We are also leaders in real estate consulting and development. We provide full service consultation which includes: creating financials, searching for real estate opportunities, and researching the best option for financing. We take you from the opportunity to the closing.


We have an intimate understanding of the urban marketplace which allows us to recognize opportunities and generate value for our customers and investor clients.


Our total facility management team is committed to providing our institutional, high net worth and retail clients tailored investment services to suit their unique objectives and goals. We dedicate time to listen to and understand each client's needs and further weigh their risk appetite in order to offer distinct investment solutions. We strive to provide clients with the superior returns on investment, while keeping in line with their personal and risk preferences, and income targets, all the while employing international standard risk management practices.


Our major total asset management services include:

  • Machinery overhauls and part replacement
  • Total support consolidated maintenance
  • Inventory analysis and control
  • InfoTech Solutions
  • Software Solutions
  • Equipment maintenance, planning, scheduling & tracking
  • Minimizing downtime of essential equipment
  • Maximum purchasing efficiency
  • Process improvement & enhancements
  • Supply chain management
